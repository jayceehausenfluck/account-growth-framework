# Renewal Motion Playbook

Designed for mid-market / enterprise SaaS accounts.

## Timeline Overview

**120 Days Before Renewal**
- Confirm contract details
- Revalidate stakeholders
- Review product adoption metrics
- Identify renewal risks

**90 Days Before Renewal**
- Align on value delivered
- Reconfirm business goals
- Surface budget alignment

**60 Days Before Renewal**
- Formal renewal conversation
- Address objections
- Lock in commercial terms

**30 Days Before Renewal**
- Finalize contract
- Secure signature
- Identify expansion paths

---

## Leading Risk Indicators
- Low product engagement
- Executive sponsor disengaged
- Budget changes
- Competitive evaluation signals
- Open critical support tickets

---

## Renewal Conversation Framework

1. Revisit original success criteria
2. Quantify value delivered
3. Align on next-year outcomes
4. Confirm investment alignment
5. Explore expansion where value is proven

---

## Metrics Impacted
- Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Churn Rate
- Expansion ARR
