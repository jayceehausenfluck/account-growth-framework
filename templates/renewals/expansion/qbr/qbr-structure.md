# QBR Structure (Executive-Friendly)

## 1) Executive Summary (1 slide)
- What changed since last quarter
- Wins + progress toward goals
- Risks + mitigations
- Next-quarter priorities

## 2) Adoption + Outcomes
- Usage trends (leading indicators)
- Business outcomes (lagging indicators)
- Support health (tickets, SLA, themes)

## 3) Value Story
- 2–3 specific wins tied to KPIs
- Customer quotes / internal feedback (if available)
- ROI narrative (even directional)

## 4) Roadmap Alignment
- Upcoming needs from customer
- Product roadmap items relevant to them
- Joint success plan for next quarter

## 5) Expansion / Growth
- Where value is proven
- What’s next (teams, use cases, add-ons)
- Decision process + timeline
