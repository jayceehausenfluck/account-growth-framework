# Renewal Motion Playbook

## Timeline (Backwards from Renewal Date)

**120 days out**
- Confirm contract basics + renewal owner
- Pull adoption/usage + support history
- Map stakeholders + power dynamics
- Identify renewal risks + mitigation plan

**90 days out**
- Value recap aligned to customer goals
- Reconfirm success plan for next term
- Budget alignment + procurement path
- Schedule exec touchpoint (if enterprise)

**60 days out**
- Commercial conversation begins
- Address risks + blockers (product, support, ROI)
- Confirm decision process + signature path

**30 days out**
- Finalize terms + paperwork
- Secure signature
- Post-renewal success plan + expansion roadmap

## Leading Risk Indicators
- Usage declining or flat
- Sponsor silent / champion changed roles
- High ticket volume / unresolved issues
- Budget tightening signals
- Competitor mentioned or security review triggered

## Renewal Conversation Flow
1. Re-anchor on original goals
2. Quantify outcomes delivered
3. Align on next-term priorities
4. Confirm investment + timeline
5. Identify expansion where value is proven

## Metrics this improves
- GRR / churn
- NRR (via expansion)
- Time-to-sign
